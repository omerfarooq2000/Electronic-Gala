

Status	     Description
Pending	The  order has been placed but payment has not yet been received.
Processing	 The order has been received and is being processed.
Shipped	The  order has been shipped and is on its way to the customer.
Delivered	 The order has been delivered to the customer.
Cancelled	 The order has been cancelled by the customer or the merchant.
Failed	     The order has failed for some reason.
